Amitabh Bachchan

Amitabh Harivansh Rai Bachchan is one of the most iconic film stars in the history of Indian cinema.His performances revolutionized the image of protagonists in the industry and Amitabh Bachchan, due to his movie roles, was popularly referred to as "the angry young man" of India.

Anil Kapoor

Anil Kapoor is an Indian actor and producer who has appeared Bollywood films, as well as international films and television series. His career has spanned almost 40 years as an actor, and as a producer since 2005. He has won many awards in his career, including two National Film Awards and six Filmfare Awards in different acting categories.

Read More

Rajesh khanna

Actor, producer, and politician Rajesh Khanna predominately worked in the Hindi cinema. After starring in 15 consecutive solo hit films from 1969 to 1971, he is called The First Superstar of Indian Film Industry. This made him one of the most successful actors in the history of Indian cinema

Read More

Pankaj Tripathy

Pankaj Tripathi is one of the renowned and talented names of the Indian Cinema who is known for his unique style of acting which connects with the audience naturally. Pankaj Tripathi is well known for his work in movies including Gunjan Saxena, Stree, Newton as well as in web series such as Sacred Games and Criminal Justice

Read More

Deepika padukone

Deepika Padukone is one of the most popular and highest-paid Indian celebrities. Padukone, the daughter of the badminton player Prakash Padukone, was born in Copenhagen and raised in Bangalore. Padukone made her Bollywood debut with Om Shanti Om and won a Filmfare Award for Best Female Debut. The 2012 movie Cocktail marked a turning point in Padukone's career, earning her praise and Best Actress nominations at several award ceremonies

Read More

Dharmendra

Dharam Singh Deol, known mononymously as Dharmendra, is an Indian actor, producer and politician who is known for his work in Hindi films. Known as the first "He-Man" of Bollywood, Dharmendra has worked in over 301 films in a career spanning over six decades.

Read More

Aishwarya Rai Bachchan

Aishwarya Rai Bachchan is an Indian actress and the winner of the Miss World 1994 pageant. Primarily known for her work in Hindi and Tamil films, she has established herself as one of the most popular and influential celebrities in India

Read More

Anupam Kher

Anupam Kher is an Indian actor and the former Chairman of Film and Television Institute of India. In a long career featuring in over 530 films, predominantly in Hindi cinema, and many plays, Kher is considered one of the most versatile film actors of India

Read More

Allu Arjun

Allu Arjun is an actor, producer, director, and dancer who works in the South Indian cinema industry. When it comes to South Indian cinema, he is one of the first names that come to mind. His films are consistently superhits and are known among the greatest Telugu films. He is on the list of the highest-earning actors in the Tollywood industry

Read More

Prabhas

Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju, known mononymously as Prabhas, is an Indian actor who works predominantly in Telugu cinema. One of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema, Prabhas has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list three times since 2015 based on his income and popularity.

Read More

Govinda

Govinda is an Indian film actor, comedian, and former politician. This star is known for his freestyle dancing skills. Govinda's first film was 1986's Ilzaam, and he has appeared in over 165 Hindi films. Govinda had lead roles in several commercially successful comedy films, including Aankhen, Raja Babu, Coolie No. 1, Hero No. 1 and Haseena Maan Jaayegi

Read More

Madhuri Dixit

Madhuri Dixit Nene, popularly known as Madhuri Dixit, is a popular Bollywood Indian actress; who has also worked as a producer, host, and television personality. She is one of the most popular actresses in Bollywood and Indian cinema. Madhuri Dixit, one of the most beautiful actresses across Indian cinema and television, has worked in more than 70 Bollywood movies
